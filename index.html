AI PING PONG GAME






Instruction

  1. First keep your laptop screen straight
  2. Move yourself approximately 3-4 feets away from the laptop
  3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and decending manner, a red dot should appear on your wrist
  4. Now press the play button, again Move yourself 3-4 feets away from the laptop
  5. The red paddle is your paddle, it will move as per your wrist movement
